Favourite Movies

Good Will Hunting


Director-Gus Van Sant

Good Will Hunting is an inspirational movie about a boy who works as a janitor at MIT. He's an undiscovered genius at mathematics which comes in light to his professor. The professor wants him to reach his potential but he gets in conflict and gets arrested. He's let out on a provision only if he goes to therapy. But after several therapists fail to talk to him, the professor calls his old friend to speak to him. It's a beautiful and touching life lesson about how the human mind works and the kindness of a stranger turned friend.

Black Swan


Director-Darren Aronofsky

The black swan casts a light on neurotic perfectionism through the eyes of a ballerina, Nina. When her company decides to produce an opening of Swan Lake, Nina fits perfectly in the role of the white swan while a new girl symbolizes the black swan. However nina's strive to perfection attitude for the role of white swan emerges the darker side of her, inducing her mind. to play tricks and hallucinate.The movies brilliant cinematography helps to capture the psychological turmoil.

Whiplash


Director-Damien Chazelle

Whiplash portrays the anguish of the obsessed artist. Andrew is selected as a drummer in a music cosservatiory. But his teacher Terrence Fletecher has an eccentric and a cut throat personality who wants to push his students to greatness. There is a fine line shown here between the pursuit of greatness and madness. However Andrew sabotages his teacher's career due to the emotional abuse but that he never strays from the path of being the greatest.The pacing, tension, disturbing elements tie it all together.
