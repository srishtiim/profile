Favourite Movies

Good Will Hunting


Director-Gus Van Sant
Good Will Hunting is an inspirational movie about a boy who works as a janitor at MIT. He's an undiscovered genius at mathematics which comes in light to his professor. The professor wants him to reach his potential but he gets in conflict and gets arrested. He's let out on a provision only if he goes to therapy. But after several therapists fail to talk to him, the professor calls his old friend to speak to him. It's a beautiful and touching life lesson about how the human mind works and the kindness of a stranger turned friend.
