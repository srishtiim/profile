Favourite Movies

Oppenheimer

Oppenheimer was an intense and brilliant life story portraying the American Prometheus, Robert J Oppenheimer, . With a brilliant but long pacing, the movie is captivating till the very end with sublime background music. The storyline and monumental significance of each scene can be felt till the very end when it leaves its audience with marvel. Directed by Christopher Nolan, its a paragon of excellence. The fact it was released as the same day as Barbie made the whole affair more memorable and created a buzz like no other.

Good Will Hunting
Director-Gus Van Sant


Good Will Hunting is an inspirational movie about a boy who works as a janitor at MIT. He's an undiscovered genius at mathematics which comes in light to his professor. The professor wants him to reach his potential but he gets in conflict and gets arrested. He's let out on a provision only if he goes to therapy. But after several therapists fail to talk to him, the professor calls his old friend to speak to him. It's a beautiful and touching life lesson about how the human mind works and the kindness of a stranger turned friend.

Black Swan
Director-Darren Aronofsky


The black swan casts a light on neurotic perfectionism through the eyes of a ballerina, Nina. When her company decides to produce an opening of Swan Lake, Nina fits perfectly in the role of the white swan while a new girl symbolizes the black swan. However nina's strive to perfection attitude for the role of white swan emerges the darker side of her, inducing her mind. to play tricks and hallucinate.The movies brilliant cinematography helps to capture the psychological turmoil.

Inception


Director-Christopher Nolan

One of Christopher Nolan's masterpices that make us question the essence our reality and how we percieve it. The protagonist of the movie is a thief who has mastered the art of stealing people's secrets through their minds by dream sharing technology. However this has ruined his life at the cost of being exceptional at his job. He is offered a chance at redemption by doing an almost impossible task by planting an idea in someone''s mind. However a vicious opponent in his own mind threatens the job at every step and the lives of his friends. An intense masterpiece that captivates the audience with its tenacious storyline.

Whiplash


Director-Damien Chazelle

Whiplash portrays the anguish of the obsessed artist. Andrew is selected as a drummer in a music cosservatiory. But his teacher Terrence Fletecher has an eccentric and a cut throat personality who wants to push his students to greatness. There is a fine line shown here between the pursuit of greatness and madness. However Andrew sabotages his teacher's career due to the emotional abuse but he never strays from the path of being the greatest which is shown in the final piece performed, Caravan.The pacing, tension and disturbing elements tie it all together.

Interstellar


Director-Christopher Nolan

This mind blowing film by Christopher Nolan encapsulates the human survival instinct. The plot revolves around the search for habitable planet before the human race faces extinction due to resources failure. A former Nasa pilot is asked to lend his expertise and lead the mission and to achieve this by going through a wormwole in outer space. However time is of the essense here as every second on Earth leads to years going by in space.The stunning visuals, emotional depth, heart-wrenching and brilliant storyline is truly a masterpiece to watch. It shows that"love is the only thing that transcends space and time".
