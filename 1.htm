Drawings Completed By Me

This painting was made by me in 2018 inspied by the backdrop of kolkata.
It portrays the beauty of rustic kolkata through watercolours.
In the background a tram can be seen of which the tram lines can be seen
even today in every part of kolkata. The gloomy and mystique background
has been set to identify the place with a sense of mystery and beauty.
